\textbf{FACILITIES}

Utah State University is an R1 research University in Logan, UT. This project will be supported by the computer science department, which offers Bachelors, Masters, and Doctoral degrees in computer science and has particular expertise in computer science education, artificial intelligence, computer vision, data mining, data visualization, database systems, and cybersecurity. Faculty and graduate students in the department have access to office and lab spaces, administrative staff, and all necessary computing equipment. Students that are part of the research project will also have access to private meeting rooms which they can reserve when needed.
