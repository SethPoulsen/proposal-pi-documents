\textbf{FACILITIES}

Utah State University is an R1 research University in Logan, UT. This project will be supported by the School of Computing, which offers Bachelors, Masters, and Doctoral degrees in computer science and has particular expertise in computer science education, artificial intelligence, computer vision, data mining, data visualization, database systems, and cybersecurity. Faculty and graduate students in the department have access to office and lab spaces, administrative staff, and all necessary computing equipment. Dr. Poulsen's PhD students also have access to a dedicated lab space each with their own desk, and a private meeting room that they can use for meetings and interviews.
